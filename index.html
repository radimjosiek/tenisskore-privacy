PRIVACY POLICY - TENISSKORE

Last updated: December 19, 2024

1. DATA COLLECTION

TenisSkore app DOES NOT COLLECT, STORE, or SHARE any personal data. The app does not access any data except tennis match scores.

2. WHAT THE APP DOES

  • Allows you to track tennis match scores
  • Stores scores ONLY locally on your Apple Watch
  • No data is sent anywhere
  • Data is deleted when you close the app or reset

3. WHAT THE APP DOES NOT COLLECT

The app DOES NOT collect:

  • Name, email, phone number
  • Location or GPS data
  • Health data
  • Contacts
  • Photos or media
  • Any personal data

4. THIRD PARTIES

The app does not communicate with any servers or third-party services. All data remains only on your device.

5. CHILDREN

The app is safe for all age groups including children.

6. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

This policy may be updated. You will be informed of changes through app updates.
